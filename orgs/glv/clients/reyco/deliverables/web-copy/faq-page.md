Lake Superior's shoreline stretches more than 2,700 kilometres in Ontario alone. For boaters, anglers, and outdoor equipment owners across the Algoma district, that means a lot of hull hours, a lot of seasonal transitions, and a lot of questions before you buy, book service, or order a part.

We answer the most common ones below. If yours isn't here, call us or stop by — we're in the Sault and we know Northern Ontario conditions.

### About Reyco Marine

**What does Reyco Marine sell?**

We sell new and used boats, outboard motors, marine accessories, power equipment, and outdoor power products. Our new product lines include Princecraft boats, Mercury outboards, EZGO electric vehicles, Toro outdoor power, Cub Cadet, Echo, Minn Kota trolling motors, Humminbird fish finders, Cannon downriggers, and Hisun utility vehicles. We also stock Easy Hauler boat trailers. Call or visit for current inventory — stock changes seasonally.

**Are you a dealer or a retailer?**

We are an authorised dealer for all brands we carry. That means factory-trained service technicians, genuine OEM parts, and warranty support backed directly by the manufacturer — not a third-party shop.

**How long has Reyco Marine been in business?**

Reyco Marine has been serving Sault Ste. Marie and the Algoma district for more than 40 years. Casey and Aaron lead the operation today, and most of our service team has been here for years. You're not dealing with a revolving door of technicians.

### Service & Repairs

**Do you service equipment you didn't sell?**

Yes. We service outboard motors, boats, and outdoor power equipment regardless of where you bought them. Bring us your Mercury, Toro, Cub Cadet, or EZGO and we will diagnose, repair, and return it properly. The only exception is warranty work, which requires dealer authorisation from the original seller.

**How long does service usually take?**

Most service appointments — seasonal tune-ups, oil changes, propeller work — take 2 to 5 business days once your unit is in our shop. Major repairs or parts-order situations take longer. Spring is our busiest period. If you want your boat ready for the walleye opener on the third Saturday in May, book your service in April. Waiting until May puts you in a longer queue.

**Can I drop off my boat or equipment without an appointment?**

You can drop it off, but booking ahead means we can schedule your work sooner. Walk-ins go into the queue behind pre-booked appointments. Call ahead and we'll give you a realistic timeline before you make the drive.

**Do you do in-water or on-site service calls?**

We service equipment brought to our facility. We do not offer mobile on-water service calls. If your boat won't start at the dock, the most reliable option is to trailer it in.

### Parts

**Do you carry parts in stock?**

Lynn and Ron manage our parts counter and we stock a broad range of common service parts for the brands we carry — filters, impellers, propellers, belts, blades, spark plugs, anodes. For less common parts, we order from the manufacturer. Most ordered parts arrive within a few business days.

**Can I order a part without bringing my equipment in?**

Yes. Call the parts counter with your model number and serial number and we'll confirm compatibility and availability before you come in or before we order. Showing up without that information slows things down for everyone.

**Do you sell used parts?**

We carry new OEM and aftermarket parts. We do not maintain a used parts inventory.

### Seasonal Services

**Do you offer winterization?**

Yes. We winterise outboard motors, stern drives, and boats each fall. Proper winterisation in a Northern Ontario climate is not optional — water left in cooling passages freezes and cracks engine blocks. Book in September or October. By late October our schedule fills quickly.

**Do you do spring commissioning?**

Yes. Spring commissioning — flushing the system, changing gear oil, checking the impeller, inspecting the hull — is the most important service of the year. Lake Superior runs cold well into June. A neglected impeller does not give you warning before it fails.

**What's the cutoff for getting service done before the walleye opener?**

Walleye season on most Algoma district inland lakes opens the third Saturday in May. To be safe, have your boat in our shop by the last week of April. If you bring it in during the first two weeks of May, we'll do our best, but we cannot guarantee timing during peak season.

### Pricing & Financing

**Do you offer financing?**

Yes. We offer financing on new product purchases. Terms and approval depend on the lender, and rates change — which is why we don't publish them online. Talk to Casey or Aaron directly to get current options and start an application.

**Do you publish service pricing?**

Labour rates and job estimates are provided when you book or drop off your equipment. We do not publish a standard rate card online because jobs vary by model, condition, and what's found during the service. We'll give you a written estimate before we start any significant repair.

### Location & Hours

**Where are you located?**

Reyco Marine is located in Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. We serve customers across the Algoma district — from Wawa south to Espanola and east toward Sudbury. Most customers drive in, drop off their equipment, and pick up when the work is done. Visit reycomarine.com for our current address and a map before you make the trip.

**What are your hours?**

Hours vary seasonally. Spring and summer we extend our hours to handle the service rush ahead of the walleye opener and the start of lake season — that's when the schedule fills fastest. Call us or check reycomarine.com for current hours before driving in, especially in spring.

**Do you ship parts?**

We focus on in-person sales and service — Sault Ste. Marie is a drive-in market and the large majority of our customers are within a day's drive of the shop. For customers further out or with time-sensitive parts needs, call Lynn or Ron at the parts counter and we'll work out the best option.
