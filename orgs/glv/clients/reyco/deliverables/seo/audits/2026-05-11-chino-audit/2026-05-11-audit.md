### Level 6: On-Page

#### FINDING 1: /aboutus brand-confusion CTR sink (PRIORITY: HIGH)

**Evidence:** 201 impressions, 1 click, 0.5% CTR, avg position 5.3

**Root cause:** About page is ranking for competitor/brand-confusion queries:
- "reyco automotive" — 11 impressions, pos 8.9
- "superior marine" — 9 impressions, pos 5.4
- "superior marine sault" — 6 impressions, pos 5.3
- "reyco automotive" total absorbing ~26 impressions with 0 clicks

The page title and meta description are not disambiguating Reyco Marine from Reyco Automotive (auto repair) or Superior Marine (SSM competitor). Users see the result, realize it's not what they searched for, and don't click.

**Fix:** Meta description rewrite to include "Reyco Marine & Small Engine — SSM's authorized boat and motor dealer. Not Reyco Automotive." (or variant). If CTR doesn't improve by Day-30 baseline, add a visible page-level disclaimer.

**FIX-0007:** /aboutus meta description — disambiguation + brand clarity

#### FINDING 2: EZGO inventory false-intent (PRIORITY: MEDIUM)

**Evidence:** `/search/inventory/class/Generators/type/Commercial%20Generators` — 20 impressions, 0 clicks, pos 7.1

**Queries triggering this page:**
- "diesel generators sault ste. marie" — 6 impressions, pos 6.0
- "natural gas generators sault ste. marie" — 6 impressions, pos 9.7
- "génératrices diesel sault ste. marie" — 5 impressions, pos 7.0 (French variant)

**Root cause:** EZGO vehicles (golf carts, utility vehicles) contain "Commercial" and "Generator" taxonomy tags in WooCommerce, causing Google to associate this inventory filter URL with generator purchase intent. Reyco does not sell standalone generators.

**Risk:** High bounce rate from these clicks will signal poor query-to-content relevance to Google — negative SEO on a page that is otherwise irrelevant but actively indexed.

**Two paths (revenue decision for Aiden):**
- Path A: Noindex or add meta description clarifying "Reyco Marine does not sell generators — explore EZGO utility vehicles and golf carts." Kills false-intent traffic, preserves EZGO relevance.
- Path B: If Aiden wants to explore generator dealer partnership, this is market evidence of demand in SSM with zero competition.

**FIX-0008:** EZGO generator false-intent — disambiguation or dealer expansion decision (Aiden decision required)
