## Platform Windows

### Instagram
**Primary surface for @glvbuilds**

| Window | Days | EST |
|--------|------|-----|
| Morning peak | Tue–Thu | 9–11 AM |
| Lunch window | Mon–Fri | 11 AM–1 PM |
| Afternoon | Tue–Thu | 1–3 PM |

- **Best overall:** Tuesday and Wednesday, 9 AM–1 PM EST
- **Avoid:** Weekends for B2B; Friday afternoon drops off sharply
- **Reels:** Post 30–60 min before peak (9:30 AM) — algorithm amplification window
- **Stories:** Lunchtime (11 AM–1 PM) outperforms for B2B professionals browsing on break

**Note:** For marketing agency content targeting SMB owners (tradespeople checking phones mid-morning), Tue–Wed 9–10 AM EST aligns with their pre-job-site scroll window.

### LinkedIn
**Primary surface for @glvbuilds**

| Window | Days | EST |
|--------|------|-----|
| Core working hours | Tue–Thu | 11 AM–3 PM |
| Late afternoon shift (2026 trend) | Tue–Fri | 3–5 PM |
| Peak single slots | Wed + Fri | 3–4 PM |

- **Best overall:** Tuesday–Thursday, 11 AM–3 PM EST; Wednesday 4 PM is highest single slot
- **2026 shift:** Late afternoon (3–5 PM) increasingly outperforming morning; algorithm change favoring end-of-workday content
- **Avoid:** Weekends entirely; Monday morning (inbox-focused, not scroll-focused)
- **Documents/carousels:** Post slightly earlier (10–11 AM) — higher save rate during work-mode browsing
