# Mistakes Log

Append-only. One entry per incident. Written immediately after the event — not retroactively.

---

## Entry Format

```

**Was passiert:** <what happened>
**Root cause:** <underlying reason>
**Lesson:** <what to do differently>
**Code-Ref:** path/to/file.ts:LINE (if applicable)
---
```
