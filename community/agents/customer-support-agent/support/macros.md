# Support Macros

These are starter macros. Replace them during `/setup` with approved product-specific language.

## Acknowledge And Investigate

Use when the issue needs investigation and no immediate policy answer is available.

Draft:

Thank you for the details. I am going to check this against our current docs and support policy before giving you a final answer. I will follow up with the next step as soon as we have the right owner or source confirmed.

Approval: required before sending.

## How-To Answer

Use when the answer is directly supported by docs.

Draft:

Thanks for reaching out. Based on the current docs, the next step is:

1. Open the relevant product area.
2. Confirm the setting or status mentioned in the ticket.
3. Follow the documented action.

I have included the source I used below so you can verify the steps.

Approval: required before sending.

## Escalation Needed

Use when the issue involves account access, billing, legal, security, privacy, refunds, incidents, angry customers, VIP customers, or policy exceptions.

Draft:

Thanks for flagging this. This needs review by the right internal owner before we give a final answer. I have escalated it with the context you provided and will follow up after that review.

Approval: required before sending.

## Missing Documentation

Use internally when the docs or macros do not answer a repeated question.

Action:

- Create `support/kb-gaps/<ticket-id>.json`.
- Include the question, attempted sources, customer impact, and suggested doc or macro.
- Route the gap to the configured docs owner.
