# Example Support Macros

## Password Reset Email Missing

Use when a customer cannot find a password reset email and there is no sign of account compromise.

Draft:

Thanks for reaching out. Please check your spam folder and confirm that the email address on the account is correct. If the reset email still does not arrive, we can escalate this for account review.

Approval: required before sending.

Sources:

- Account access policy
- Password reset docs

## Billing Review Needed

Use when a customer asks for a refund, credit, invoice correction, charge explanation, or payment change.

Draft:

Thanks for flagging this. Billing changes need review by the billing owner before we confirm next steps. I have escalated it with the invoice context and will follow up after review.

Approval: required before sending.
